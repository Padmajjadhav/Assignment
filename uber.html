About Uber

Uber is a global ride-hailing platform that connects riders with drivers through its easy-to-use mobile app. It offers car, auto, and bike rides along with premium options for a comfortable experience.


Key Features

  • 24/7 ride availability across major cities.
  • Real-time tracking and sharing trip details.
  • Multiple payment options — card, UPI, cash.
  • In-app emergency assistance for safety.
  • Uber Rewards and loyalty points for regular users.

More Information

Founded in 2009, Uber operates in over 70 countries. It is known for its fast service, driver ratings, and route optimization technology. Uber also supports eco-friendly rides through its Green initiative.
