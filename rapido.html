Rapido - Quick & Affordable

About Rapido

Rapido is India’s largest bike taxi platform offering convenient and economical two-wheeler rides. It helps users beat traffic and save time through its quick booking system.


Key Features

  • Instant booking and fast pickup.
  • Affordable fares for short distances.
  • Available in 100+ cities across India.
  • Insurance coverage for every ride.
  • Earn as a “Captain” (driver partner).

More Information

Founded in 2015, Rapido focuses on providing last-mile connectivity with minimal wait time. It also offers Rapido Auto and Rapido Rental for extended use. The app is lightweight and easy to use.
